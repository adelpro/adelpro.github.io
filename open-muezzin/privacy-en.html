Open Muezzin Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 9, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

At Open Muezzin, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how your data is handled when using our browser extension.

What Information Do We Collect?

  • Geolocation: Used only to calculate accurate prayer times based on your current position. Your coordinates are processed locally and never sent to external servers.
  • Storage: Used to save your preferences, such as calculation method, location, and notification settings. All data remains on your device.
  • Alarms: Used to schedule prayer time reminders and Athan notifications even when the browser is closed.
  • Host Permission: https://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/* is used to convert your coordinates into a readable location (city, country) through the OpenStreetMap Nominatim API. No personal data is transmitted or stored.

How We Use Information

All permissions are strictly used for prayer time calculations and notification functionality. No data is tracked, profiled, or monetized.

Data Storage and Security

All saved data (preferences and cached coordinates) are stored securely within your browser using Chrome or Firefox storage APIs. Open Muezzin does not use remote databases or third-party analytics.

We Don't Share Your Data

Since Open Muezzin stores all information locally, no data is shared with any third party. We have no access to your information.

Children's Privacy

Open Muezzin is suitable for users of all ages. No personal data or identifiable information is collected from anyone, including children.

Third-Party Services

The only external service accessed is the OpenStreetMap Nominatim API, used solely for converting coordinates to readable locations. No personal data or identifiers are included in these requests.

Updates to this Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Any major changes will be reflected here with a new effective date. Continued use of the extension signifies acceptance of any updates.
